Game Info · All the features

The Top-Down Shooter from P0ST SCR1PT STUD10S, Shoot The Zombies, is making its way to the palm of your hand. Survive for as long as you can by shooting and smashing you way through the zombie horde to score points, set high scores, fight huge bosses, and rescue other survivors to join you in your efforts to stay alive.
